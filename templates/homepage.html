We believe in complete transparency of the press, and in the power of truth. That is why we created Fact Filter, a website that lets you browse the most popular news sites, and automatically picks out the subjective statements in any certain story and shows them to you. You can also pick out any text of any smaller publication and use our algorithm to find all subjective statements.
